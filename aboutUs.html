About Us

Mmasello Matsoi founded Mascloset Design in 2014 in Limpopo. Later, the owner relocated to the City of Gold to expand her brand and clients. Aiming to offer women distinctive and reasonably priced apparel, Mascloset Design is a clothing company.
Mascloset designs provide high-quality and fashionable finished goods. Each design is created using the preferences and personalities of our clients.

Mission


Our mission as Mascloset is to acheive the highest possible standard in the fashion industry and to provide employement opportunities in our country.

Vision


To make Mascloset the place of choice to look good and wear quality clothing.
